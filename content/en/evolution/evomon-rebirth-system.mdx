## What is the Evomon Rebirth System?

If you've been diving into the new Roblox monster-catching sensation, you've likely heard whispers about the **Evomon rebirth system**. This feature, which appears to unlock at level 20, allows you to reset your monster's level in exchange for powerful stat boosts and new abilities. Based on early player experience and community reports from launch day, this system is reminiscent of prestige mechanics found in other RPGs, but with a unique twist tailored to the world of Evomon.

The core idea is simple: you take a powerful monster, hit the "Ascend" button, and send it back to level 1. In return, your Evomon gains a significant boost to its growth rates, unlocks the ability to learn new skills, and potentially increases its talent tier. This guide will break down everything we know so far about the rebirth process, helping you decide which monsters to ascend and when.

## How to Unlock the Ascension Feature

According to gameplay footage from the launch day, the ascension quest becomes available once your player level hits 20. This is a crucial milestone, as it signals you're ready to start optimizing your team. You won't see the option before then, so focus on completing quests, battling wild monsters, and dueling NPCs like Aaron and Peter to gain XP.

Once you reach level 20, navigate to your monster's detail screen. You should see a new button labeled "Ascend" or "Rebirth." Clicking it will initiate the process, but be warned—this is a permanent decision for that specific monster. You cannot reverse a rebirth, so choose wisely.

## When Should You Rebirth Your Evomon?

Timing is everything. You shouldn't rebirth your first monster the moment you hit level 20. Here’s a simple table to help you decide:

| Scenario | Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- |
| **You have a duplicate monster.** | Rebirth is a great use for your second-best copy. |
| **Your monster has SSS talent.** | This is a prime candidate. The boost will be massive. |
| **You're struggling with a boss.** | Level up a different monster first. Rebirth weakens you temporarily. |
| **You have plenty of fruit (XP items).** | You can quickly level the monster back up after rebirth. |

The best strategy is to stockpile experience fruits before you rebirth. This way, you can immediately feed your level 1 monster back up to a respectable level, minimizing downtime.

## A Step-by-Step Guide to Rebirth

Here’s a practical, step-by-step guide based on the available information:

1.  **Reach Player Level 20:** Complete daily quests, battle wild monsters, and duel NPCs.
2.  **Select Your Candidate:** Choose a high-talent monster (S or SSS) that you have a duplicate of, or one you're willing to level again.
3.  **Stockpile Resources:** Gather at least 20-30 experience fruits and some gold.
4.  **Initiate Ascension:** Open the monster's menu and click "Ascend."
5.  **Confirm the Rebirth:** The monster will return to level 1, and its stats will reset.
6.  **Re-Level Quickly:** Use your stored fruits to feed the monster back up to a high level.
7.  **Check the Results:** Compare the new stats and growth rate to the pre-rebirth values.

## Common Questions About the Evomon Rebirth System

### Can I rebirth any monster?
Based on early access, it seems any monster can be rebirthed once you unlock the feature. However, it's best to focus on high-tier monsters like SSS or Prismatic variants.

### Does rebirth change a monster's type or moves?
No, the monster's base type (e.g., Fire, Grass) stays the same. However, it can learn new skills as it levels up again, and it may have access to moves it couldn't learn before.

### How many times can I rebirth a single Evomon?
This is not yet confirmed. Some games allow multiple rebirths (Rebirth 1, Rebirth 2, etc.), each offering greater bonuses. We'll need to wait for more player experience to confirm the exact limits in Evomon.

### Is the rebirth system worth it for casual players?
If you're just playing for fun, you don't need to engage with the rebirth system. It's designed for players who want to optimize their team and tackle the hardest content. The game is perfectly enjoyable without it.
