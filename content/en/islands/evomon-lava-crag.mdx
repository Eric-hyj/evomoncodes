- **Always carry heat-resistant potions.** The ambient temperature can drain your health quickly.

- **Avoid traveling at night.** Nocturnal creatures in the **Evomon Lava Crag** are more aggressive and numerous.
- **Mark your map.** The volcanic landscape can look very similar, making it easy to get lost.
