## Advanced Tips for Your Chosen Starter

Once you've picked the **evomon best starter** (Bubble), here are some advanced tips to maximize your potential.

### Understanding Talents and Natures

Your starter's potential isn't just about its type. Each monster has **Talents** (stat bonuses based on rank like S or A) and **Natures** (a positive and negative stat modifier).

- **Talents:** A higher rank (S, SS, SSS) gives you a larger flat bonus to your stats. You can reroll these bonuses to focus on key stats like Attack or Defense.
- **Natures:** You can reroll a monster's nature to get a +10% boost to a primary stat (e.g., Attack) at the cost of -10% to a less important stat (e.g., Special Defense).

For Bubble, focus on boosting its **Special Attack** and **Speed** to maximize its water-type damage output.

### The Secret EXP Farm

One player shared a powerful method for gaining massive amounts of EXP in the mid-game. This is crucial for evolving your starter and building a strong team.

1.  **Farm Boss Tokens:** Go to an area where you can quickly defeat bosses (e.g., using your water-type starter against fire bosses).
2.  **Exchange for Fruits:** Take the boss tokens to the **Exchange Merchant** in the main city.
3.  **Buy Large EXP Fruits:** Exchange your tokens for large EXP fruits, which grant 10,000 EXP each.

This method allows you to level up your monsters incredibly fast, bypassing the slow grind of wild battles.
