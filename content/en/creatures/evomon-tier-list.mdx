## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the best starter in Evomon?**
Based on the current **evomon tier list**, Bubble is the best starter. Its water typing gives you a strong advantage against the first two zones and the world boss, which is fire-type.

**How do I get S-rank or triple S creatures?**
You can get high-rank creatures from eggs, the Battle Pass, or by catching wild monsters. Using a prismatic ball guarantees a triple S rank, but regular balls can also yield high ranks. One player reported catching a triple S creature with a regular ball.

**What’s the fastest way to level up my rank?**
Complete quests from Lillian on each island for 50 rank EXP each. Also, fill your index by capturing new creatures. Each new entry gives 50 EXP. Finally, use the EXP challenge area in Petal Pond with your free daily tickets.

**Are shiny creatures stronger than normal ones?**
Shiny creatures appear to be cosmetic only. Community reports suggest they don’t have better stats, but they can have high ranks. Focus on rank and nature rather than shininess for competitive play.
